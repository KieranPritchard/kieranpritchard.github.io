My iDEA Awards

Date Published: 14/02/2025

Topics: Self-Paced LearningCertifacates

This page takes a look at the certifacates I have earned with my time going through the different awards on iDEA, iDEA is a online learning platform that is described as the digital equivant of the Duke Of Edinburgh awards.

My Bronze Award

The bronze award was the first I earned, it was structured around completing short miniture units. These units were broken up into four different catagories: worker badges, citzen badges, maker badges, and entrepreneur badges. In order to get the bronze I needed to complete 50 points worth of badges under the four catagories however, due to the length of them I found I got the bronze award within days of joining.

My Sliver Award

The sliver award was similer to the bronze, complete a set criteria of badges across now five different catagories. However the badges to complete this award require the user to complete more longer and more involved tasks. I found the gamer badges on this award to be my favorite as they required completing different puzzles to complete. Unfortunately, my learning with the platform stopped here, because the team behind havent launched gold award yet. However, you can start completing some of the new badges for gold.
