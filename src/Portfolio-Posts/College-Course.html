My College Course

Date Published: 21/02/2025

Topics: Education

A complete overview of my time on bournemouth and poole colleges, "T-Level Foundation in Digital" course. This page takes a brief look at each of the different units these are: online world, technology systems, web design and portfolio devlopement, digital graphics, and networking. In the future i am planning on expanding this page to have sub pages on each topic and assignment.

Online World

This is unit is based around how technology is used in our day to day life. The unit covers many topics from cloud computing to online software, the lessons for this unit also had me working hands on with new tools and software. The unit one of two that is graded based on an exam score, as supposed to assignments like the other units. As you see the images i have chosen are the websites we used for revision, but i have also included screenshots of some of the software i was introduced to.

Technology Systems

This unit like online world is based around a how technology is used in the world however, unlike online world this unit is more about the technology works. This is the other exam unit that is based around an exam, not assignments. In the images below i have given you a view into the ways of revision that both exams use. The first are text books with examstyle questions, the other is a website called KnowItAllNinja.

Web Devlopement & Design

This unit of the course has been my favorite by far. this is because all the work and assignments we have been working on works towards building our portfolio websites. Im planning on making a subpage in the future that explores this further and in more detail, there alot to unpack with this unit. The images in the carousel are taken from the delevlopment of the website however, once im allowed to i will include other work samples.

Digital Graphics

This unit has been the most challenging for me, because i had to learn tools i had never used before. these tools include Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. While my work hasn't many samples yet from this unit, while we were getting started we practised ai image generation with canva. so until i have more to share here are some of my samples of ai artwork.

Networking

This unit has been most hands on of the whole course, other then the web design unit. In this unit not only do have we learnt the theory behind networks. We have also have built our own ethernet cables however at the moment we are working with virtualisation and windows server. Like the previous two units this section will have its own expanded subpage because there to to much to cover with this on and to little space to cover all of it. the images i have given are a sneak peek at what i have done with it.
