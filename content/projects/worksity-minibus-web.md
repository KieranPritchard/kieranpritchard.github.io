## Overview

Team project with a practical brief: make information easy to find on phones, keep branding consistent, and ship something a non-technical client could maintain.

## What worked well

- Early content outline before pixels (fewer layout rewrites)  
- Shared component styling conventions for spacing and type scale  
- Clear handoff notes for future edits  

## Team notes

Communication was treated as a deliverable: weekly demos, decision logs, and a short README for the repo.
